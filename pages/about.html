About 2K IQ Lab

2K IQ Lab was built for players who are tired of guessing. Most losses in competitive runs are not random; they come from repeated patterns such as poor role balance, bad spacing decisions, rushed possessions, and weak matchup planning. This project turns those patterns into simple, fast diagnostics that can be used before queueing or right after a game.

The site combines five focused tools in one lightweight static experience: a teammate scanner, a loss analyzer, a build counter planner, an animation optimizer, and a squad chemistry evaluator. Every report is generated in the browser from your input and transparent heuristic rules, so you can learn from the output and adjust your approach immediately.

Our design philosophy is practical and respectful. Free mode gives everyone access to core recommendations and supports the project with ad placements. Pro mode removes ads and unlocks more detailed guidance, stronger sharing options, and additional planning features for serious players.

2K IQ Lab is intentionally static: no account requirement, no server dependency, and no hidden matchmaking logic. You keep control of your data inside your browser. That keeps the experience fast, easy to deploy, and simple to maintain.
